About Us

The University has two women hostels - Krishna Hostel and Kaveri Hostel, to accommodate approximately 300 students.These two hostels are located in the University campus. The hostel authorities always facilitate to create an environment for the students to study, do well in their academics and focus on their career and future.



Why we are best
for you

These hostels provide a safe, secure and clean environment for the students to grow, learn and mature in the society away from their own homes.

Rooms

All rooms are on twin/triple sharing basis and are equipped with individual beds, chairs, built-in cupboards and study tables.
